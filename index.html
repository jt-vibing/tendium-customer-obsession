Root Causes

Three fractures

1
Definitions & Types
What is a “customer”? A paying company? A trial user? An org number? Every team defines it differently.
2
Silos & Confusion
Hugo spends 5 hours every Monday manually joining data from Younium, PostHog, and Garba. The answer exists — it’s just spread across 15+ systems.
3
Sources of Data
90K procurement awards + 14K pipeline, 100K company groups, billing, usage, call recordings — all disconnected. Org number is the join key nobody uses.

One customer. One truth.
All teams.
