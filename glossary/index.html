Lead PROCUREMENT Proposed
Proposed: An organization that has appeared in procurement pipeline data (active tender or previous award) and is not yet a Tendium customer.
How teams define this today
Sales "Anyone in HubSpot with an open deal"

Company Group ALLABOLAG Proposed
Proposed: A group of companies linked by parent org_nr from Allabolag data (100k companies mapped). Used for warm lead identification ‚Äî if one subsidiary is a customer, the whole group is warm.
How teams define this today
Sales "We don't track company groups ‚Äî each deal is standalone"

Active Customer POSTHOG Debated
Proposed: A customer with at least one user login + one core action (search, filter, or BidFlow activity) in the last 7 days. Measured via PostHog. Current: 5,000 WAC.
How teams define this today
Sales "Has logged in recently" (no specific timeframe)

Leadership "Paying and not churned"
Onboarding Complete POSTHOG Proposed
Proposed: A customer that has completed: (1) first login, (2) created at least one tender filter, (3) viewed at least one tender detail page. Measured within first 14 days.
How teams define this today
CS "Completed the onboarding call and setup session"
Product "No formal definition ‚Äî we track first login only"

Health Score COMPOSITE Proposed
Proposed: Weighted score (0‚Äì100) combining: Product activity (PostHog, 40%), CS engagement (Garba meetings + HubSpot tickets, 30%), Contract signals (Younium renewal date + payment history, 30%).

Churn YOUNIUM Debated
Proposed: A customer whose subscription status changed to "cancelled" or "expired" in Younium with no active replacement subscription.
