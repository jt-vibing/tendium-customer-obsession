These wireframes show the proposed user experience for the Tendium Customer Graph. All data is realistic but mocked ‚Äî based on Adlibris (Jens's Sales Graph Explorer example) and typical Nordic customers. The goal is to validate the UX/UI approach before building with real data.

Wireframe Pages

1
Customer Search
Multi-tiered search interface with keyword, natural language, and semantic search modes. Filter by tier, ARR, health, and sector.
  • 3 search modes (Keyword, Natural Language, Semantic)
  • Real-time results with match reasons
  • Filter pills for advanced filtering
  • Example queries for each mode
2
Customer Profile
Unified view of customer data from 7 sources: Procurement, Younium, PostHog, Garba, HubSpot, Postmark, Company Groups.
  • 5 tabs: Overview, Procurement, Usage, Calls, Punch Cards
  • Key metrics: ARR, Contract end, NRR, Win rate
  • Recent activity timeline
  • Health score indicator
3
Sales Punch Card
AI-generated intelligence brief for sales calls. 5 sections with procurement insights, relationship status, usage signals, and recommended actions.
  • Procurement profile (win rate, bid volume, sectors)
  • Current relationship (ARR, tier, expansion gap)
  • Usage signals (adoption, health score)
  • Recent activity (awards, calls, milestones)
  • Recommended actions (upsell, renewal, monitoring)
4
CS Health Brief
Hugo's Monday spreadsheet replacement. AI-generated health assessment with churn signals, usage trends, and proactive CS actions.
  • Customer health score (computed metric)
  • Usage trends (30/60/90 day analysis)
  • Churn signals (positive/warning/risk indicators)
  • Procurement activity as growth indicator
  • Recommended CS actions
