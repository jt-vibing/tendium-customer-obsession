THREE FRACTURES

Definitions & Types AMBER

Sales says organic leads are 20% of pipeline. Marketing says 5%. CS can't agree on "activated." Leadership has three different ARR figures depending on which system they pull from.

Every alignment meeting degenerates into a definition argument instead of a decision.

Silos & Confusion RED

CS analyzed 191 Garba calls in isolation. Hugo manually assembles data from 3+ systems. Product ships features without knowing which customers use them.

Decisions made on partial data. Nobody trusts anyone else's numbers.

Sources of Data RED

9+ systems with no shared join key. Stripe feeds Younium which feeds HubSpot which feeds reports. Each intermediary adds noise and loses fidelity.

A customer in billing is a different entity than a customer in product analytics.

These three fractures are parallel, not sequential. The Tendium Customer Graph addresses all three simultaneously.

THE METRICS TRIANGLE

100M
SEK New ARR

3x growth from ~53M base. ~2,825 new companies needed at Core ACV (35,400 SEK/yr). Split: 33M SE + 33M NO/FI + 33M DE.

engagement → revenue companies → money
WAC
Weekly Active Companies

Defined by Find engagement -- at least one user viewed, saved, or dismissed a tender that week. Decomposes: new WAC (activation), retained WAC (retention), WAC by tier (expansion signal). Replaces WAU.

PCM
Paying Companies by Market

Count of companies on active paid plans, per market (SE/NO/FI/DE). WAC:PCM ratio = trial conversion health. ARR:PCM = effective ACV. "100 deals in Germany" = PCM-DE target.

conversion health
NRR 120% demoted to diagnostic beneath ARR -- it explains WHY ARR is growing (expansion vs. new logos) but isn't top-line at 3x hyper-growth where new logo velocity dominates.
