A: Definitions & Types AMBER

Sales says organic leads are 20% of pipeline. Marketing says 5%. CS can't agree on what "activated" means. Leadership pulls ARR from three different systems and gets three different numbers. Every alignment meeting degenerates into a definition argument instead of a decision.

B: Silos & Confusion RED

CS analyzed 191 Garba calls in complete isolation from the product team. Hugo spends hours weekly assembling data from Younium, HubSpot, and PostHog into spreadsheets that are stale by Friday. Product ships features without knowing which customer segments use them. Decisions are made on partial data, and nobody trusts anyone else's numbers.

C: Sources of Data RED

Nine systems, no shared join key. A customer in Younium (billing) is a different entity than a customer in PostHog (analytics) is a different entity than a company in HubSpot (CRM). Stripe feeds Younium which feeds HubSpot which feeds reports -- each intermediary abstracts away fidelity. The procurement data that powers the entire product lives in its own world.

These fractures are parallel, not sequential. The Tendium Customer Graph addresses all three simultaneously.

100M
SEK NEW ARR

3x growth from ~53M base. At Core ACV of 35,400 SEK/yr, this requires ~2,825 new paying companies across all markets. Not total -- NEW added in 2026.

WAC
WEEKLY ACTIVE COMPANIES

Defined by Find engagement -- at least one user viewed, saved, or dismissed a tender that week. Decomposes into new WAC (activation), retained WAC (retention), and WAC by tier (expansion signal). Replaces WAU -- we measure company-level value, not individual logins.

PCM
PAYING COMPANIES BY MARKET

Count of companies on active paid plans, per market (SE/NO/FI/DE). The bridge between engagement and revenue: WAC:PCM ratio = trial conversion health. ARR:PCM = effective ACV (expansion quality). "100 deals in Germany" = a PCM-DE pipeline target.

How these connect: WAC without PCM growth means trials aren't converting. PCM without ARR growth means accounts aren't expanding. The three metrics form a system -- each constrains and informs the others. NRR (120% target) demoted to diagnostic beneath ARR.
