Key Insights
0.1% Signal
Procurement Data spans all 5 stages — the only source covering the entire journey. It’s the acquisition signal (prospect identification), the retention signal (bid volume = business health), and the referral signal (happy high-activity customers). No competitor has this data.
The Gap
Acquire is the most fragmented stage — 7 tools, none connected, no company-level visitor identification. 3 tools (GA, Segment, PostHog) overlap on website tracking. The journey from “unknown visitor” to “identified prospect” is a black box.
Multi-Stage Tools
PostHog spans 4 stages (Acquire → Activate → Retain → Referral) — the natural cross-stage connection point. Garba (call recordings) is Phase 1 — valuable for sentiment but raw transcripts need LLM processing.
